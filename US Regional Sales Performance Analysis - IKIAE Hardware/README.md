# US Regional Sales Performance Analysis - IKIAE Hardware

Description: This project was developed during the MSIB (Magang dan Studi Independen Bersertifikat) program at RevoU. The analysis addresses IKIAE Hardware's stagnant revenue growth, which only saw a 0.5% YoY increase between 2019 and 2020. By analyzing regional sales data, customer behavior, and product performance, the team identified underperforming sectors and provided strategic recommendations to accelerate revenue growth.

👥 Project Team & Role
This project was a collaborative effort led by a General Project Leader, supported by two specialized PICs (Data Analyst & Data Visualization).

My Role: PIC Data Analyst As the Lead Data Analyst, I was responsible for the end-to-end data processing and analytical framework. My key contributions included:

Complex Data Merging: Integrated 6 distinct datasets (Sales Orders, Customers, Store Locations, Products, Regions, and Sales Team) into a unified analytical master file.

Data Cleaning & Prep: Managed data integrity in Google Sheets, ensuring accurate date formatting, handling null values, and validating sales calculations.

Trend Identification: Performed deep-dive analysis to find the root causes of the 0.5% stagnant growth by analyzing sales cycles and regional performance.

Actionable Insights: Discovered key correlations between specific sales teams and regional performance to provide data-backed growth strategies.

Cross-Functional Collaboration: Provided the foundational data and logic for the PIC Data Visualization to build the interactive dashboard.

🛠️ Tools & Skills
Tools: Google Sheets, Canva, PowerBI.

Skills: Multi-table Data Merging (Join/VLOOKUP), Sales Trend Analysis, Regional Profiling, Data Cleaning, Team Leadership, Stakeholder Communication.

📂 Datasets (US Regional Sales Data)
The analysis utilized 6 core sheets from the US Regional Sales master file:

Sales Orders: Transactional data (Orders, Unit Price, Cost, dsb).

Customers: Profiles of purchasing entities.

Products: Catalog details of IKIAE Hardware items.

Store Locations: Geographic data of physical stores.

Regions: Regional grouping for performance comparison.

Sales Team: Performance and identification of sales personnel.

💡 Key Findings & Strategic Recommendations
Finding: Identified specific regions where sales were high but profit margins were thin due to high operational costs.

Recommendation: Proposed a re-allocation of sales resources to high-margin regions to overcome the 0.5% growth bottleneck.
