# Credit Risk Analysis & Sales Optimization Simulation-exercise

Credit Card Approval Analysis - XYZ Company
Description: This project focuses on analyzing the creditworthiness of customers for XYZ Company, a fictional credit firm catering to individual needs. By evaluating historical payment behavior and demographic data, the project identifies key customer characteristics that define a "good" or "bad" credit profile. The primary objective is to help the company minimize default risks while increasing sales revenue by targeting the most reliable customer segments.

Tools: Google Sheets, Google Slides & Tableau Public

Skills: Data cleaning (handling missing values & duplicates), feature engineering (defining target variables), demographic profiling, Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), and business analytic.

Outputs:

Interactive Tableau Dashboard: Visual analysis of income distribution, employment status, and asset ownership in relation to credit status.
Business Presentation (Google Slides): A strategic summary outlining the ideal customer profile and actionable recommendations to optimize credit approval policies.

Datasets:

application_record.csv - Demographic profile of applicants (Income, Education, Asset Ownership, etc.).

Fields: 18

Records: 438,557

credit_record.csv - Monthly credit payment history.

Fields: 3

Records: 1,048,575
